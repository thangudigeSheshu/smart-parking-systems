Origin Story
It started with a daily struggle for campus parking.

As B.Tech students, finding a parking spot before morning lectures was always a gamble. We realized that while our coursework was teaching us to build smart systems, our own campus parking was entirely manual and inefficient.

We decided to change that for our Capstone project. The idea was simple: use our coding skills to show drivers exactly where to park before they even arrive.

Built from the ground up this year, ParkMate Prime is the result of countless late-night coding sessions. Our mission: apply our engineering education to solve a real-world problem.
