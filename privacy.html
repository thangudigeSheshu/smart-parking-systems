Section 03
Security Measures
Updated: January 2026

We implement the following technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

🔐
Token Hashing
All booking tokens use XOR-seeded base-36 hashing. Raw tokens are never stored.
🛡️
No Remote Servers
All data stays in your browser's localStorage. We have no central database of your personal data.
🔍
No Third-Party Scripts
We use zero advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or social media pixels on any page.
⏱️
Session Expiry
Auth sessions auto-expire after 24 hours of inactivity to prevent unauthorized access.
🧂
Password Salting
Passwords are never stored in plaintext. Only salted hashes are kept in localStorage.
🚫
Zero Cross-Site Sharing
localStorage data cannot be accessed by any other domain or subdomain. Fully sandboxed.
